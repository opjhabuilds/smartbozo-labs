SmartBozo Labs

Field notes from an independent lab building with AI, for business and consumers.

SmartBozo Labs is a place to explore, experiment, and ship. We make small, useful software, sometimes for fun, sometimes for revenue, always to learn. Some products graduate into businesses. Others teach us what not to build next. Either way is fine by us.

§ 01 / Operating principles

How the lab works.

i.

Explore openly.

Most ideas die. That is fine. Curiosity is the cheapest form of research, and the best filter is shipping. We chase questions before answers and stay honest about what we do not yet know.

ii.

Experiment fast.

Build the smallest version. Put it in front of real people. Watch what happens. Every product here runs through a six axis validation playbook before code is written. Score below the line, the idea is killed early and cheaply.

iii.

Build with intent.

When something earns its way through validation, we build it properly. Design that respects the user, code that does not pretend to be magic, AI used as a tool and not as a costume.
